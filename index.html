Overview of the gdsc program 
The Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) program is a Google-backed initiative that empowers  students
around the world to learn about and contribute to various Google technologies and open-source projects.
 GDSCs are community groups run by students, for students, with a focus on fostering collaboration, 
skill development, and networking. They organize workshops, hackathons, and events to help 
students gain practical experience and connect with like-minded peers and industry professionals.
 The program covers a wide range of topics, including Android development, machine learning, cloud computing, and more.
